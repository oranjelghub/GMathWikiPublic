> [!quote] Definition: Diagonalizable matrices
> Let $A \in M_{n\times n}(\mathbb{\mathbb{C}})$, we say that $A$ is diagonalizable if and only if $A$ is similar to some diagonal matrix $D\in M_{n\times n}(\mathbb{\mathbb{C}})$.
