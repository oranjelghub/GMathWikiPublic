> [!quote] Definition: Transposing permutations
> Let $\sigma$ be some permutation of some set $X$, we say $\sigma$ is a transposition if and only if $$ \exists i,j \in X: \sigma|_{X\backslash\{ i,j \}}=\text{Id}_{X\backslash\{ i,j \}}\land \sigma|_{\{ i,j \}}\neq \text{Id}_{\{ i,j \}} $$
