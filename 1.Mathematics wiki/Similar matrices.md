> [!quote] Definition: Similar matrices
> Let $F$ be a field and let $A,B \in M_{n\times n}(F)$. We say $A$ and $B$ are similar if there exists some matrix $P \in M_{n\times n}(F)$ such that $$ A=P^{-1}BP $$
