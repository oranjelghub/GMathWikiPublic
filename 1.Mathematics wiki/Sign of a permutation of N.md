> [!quote] Definition: Sign of a permutation of $\mathbb{N}_{\leq n}$
> Let $\sigma$ be a permutation of $\mathbb{N}_{\leq n}$ for some $n\in \mathbb{N}$, then we define $$ \text{sgn}:S_{n}\to \mathbb{N}:\sigma\mapsto (-1)^{\#\text{Inv}(\sigma)} $$
