> [!quote] Theorem:
> Let $\left| * \right|$ be the absolute value operator, then 
> - $|x|\geq 0\land(|x|=0\iff x=0)$ for all $x$.
> - $|x+y|\leq|x|+|y|$
> - $-y\leq x\leq y \iff y\geq |x|$ and $-|x|\leq x\leq |x|$
> - $|xy|=|x||y|$ and $|-x|=|x|$
