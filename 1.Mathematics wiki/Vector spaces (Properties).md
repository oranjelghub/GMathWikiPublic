> [!quote] Theorem: Vector spaces (Properties)
> Let $(V,+,*)$ be a vector space over some field $F$ and let $v\in V$ and $f\in F$, then $$\begin{align}
f*v=O_V &\implies f=O_F\lor v=O_V\\
-(-v)&=v\\
-I_Fv&=-v\\
-f*v&=f*-v\\
\end{align}$$
