> [!quote] Theorem:
>  Let $(V,+,*)$ be a vector space over some field $F$. $W\subseteq V$ is a vector subspace of $V$ if and only if
> 1. $W\neq \emptyset$
> 2. $\forall v,w\in W: v+w\in W$
> 3. $\forall f\in F:\forall v\in W: fv\in W$
