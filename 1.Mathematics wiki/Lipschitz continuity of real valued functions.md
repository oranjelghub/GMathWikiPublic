> [!quote] Definition:
> Let $X\subseteq \mathbb{R}$ and $f:X\to \mathbb{R}$, we say $f$ is Lipschitz continuous on $X$ with Lipschitz constant $K\in \mathbb{R}^{+}$ if and only if $$ \forall x,y\in X, \left| f(x)-f(y) \right| \leq K\left| x-y \right|  $$
